What we do

Teach

Based on member interest, we give students the tools to launch their own ideas. Learn the basics of python, design your own database, make your own Google Cardboard, understand the world of cryptocurrency. We do everything.

Connect

From New Orleans to Silicon Valley, Cookies & Code connects students to the larger tech communities. We offer internship connections, research opportunities, as well as insights from the tech world's leading professionals.

Explore

As a computer science club, we are constantly exploring the newest technologies. Get access to Tulane's Maker Space. Discover new tools for your tech project. We are dedicated to bringing our members an abundance of opportunities.
